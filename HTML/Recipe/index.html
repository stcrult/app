Ingredients

  • 200g golden caster sugar
  • 200g unsalted butter,
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk
STEPS
  1. In a large bowl, beat together ingredients until pale.
  2. Divide the mixture between the prepared tins. Bake for 20 mins.
  3. For the buttercream, stirring every 30 secs.
  4. Now melt 25g milk chocolate.
